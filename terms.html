2. Description of Service

2.1 What Receipts for YNAB Does

Receipts for YNAB is an iOS mobile application that provides:

  • Receipt Scanning: Capture or import photos of paper receipts
  • Data Extraction: Use Apple Intelligence to extract merchant names, dates, amounts, and line items
  • Receipt Management: Store and organize scanned receipts on your device
  • YNAB Integration: Sync receipt data to your YNAB account as transactions
  • Category Matching: Suggest appropriate YNAB categories for your purchases

2.2 What Receipts for YNAB Does NOT Do

The App does not:

  • Store your data on external servers (all data is on your device)
  • Access your banking or financial accounts directly
  • Provide financial advice or budgeting recommendations
  • Guarantee 100% accuracy of extracted receipt data
  • Modify or delete YNAB transactions created by other apps or sources

2.3 Requirements

To use Receipts for YNAB, you must have:

  • An iPhone running iOS 26 (releasing Fall 2025) or later
  • For AI scanning: A device with Apple Intelligence capabilities (iPhone 15 Pro, iPhone 15 Pro Max, or newer)
  • For manual entry: Any iPhone running iOS 26+
  • A valid, active YNAB account (subscription required by YNAB)
  • An internet connection for YNAB synchronization
